Our Services

PhysAssist is a software designed to help visually-impaired and blind students in learning physics related concepts. The application is designed with accessibility in mind, and places a focus on allowing these students to participate in class just as any other student would. The PhysAssist team is committed to continually and consistently updating the application with features that further benefit visually-impaired and blind learners across the world.
Some features offered by PhysAssist include:

  • Detailed explanations of physics topics with robust practice tools
  • Voice navigation features and large and accessible UI elements
  • A community-driven practice question bank where user-uploaded questions may be approved for the official bank
  • Accessible notetaking and navigation for vision-impaired users
  • "Join a class" feature which allows students to join their instructors classroom and follow along in lectures
  • Testing and exam features with Canvas integration
  • Community features to help motivate students, allowing them to connect with others who face similar struggles
  • "Adaptive Accessibility" features that allow users to customize their UI based on their limitations
